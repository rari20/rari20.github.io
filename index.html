How to Pass in School Comp Sci edition

1. Time Management

Allocating your time wisely is crucial for academic success.

2. Active Participation

Engage actively in classes to better understand the material.

3. Study Techniques

Adopt study techniques like the Pomodoro Technique, or spaced repetition for better results.

4. Seek Help

If you're struggling with a particular subject, don't hesitate to seek help from teachers, tutors, or classmates.

5. Self-care

Physical well-being is equally important; exercise, sleep, and proper nutrition can boost your academic performance.
